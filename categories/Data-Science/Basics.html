SE vs SD

The standard deviation of the sample means is the standard error.
In general, standard error is only used for confidence intervals, and standard deviation is used for everything else.

Measures of Location

Weighted Means

Sensor Data
You have a sensor giving bad data 2% of the time. It's off a bit when it does, but nothing ridiculous. How can you prevent this from completely ruining your mean? Use a weighted mean

Trimmed Mean

Inflation Rates
The Great Depression and wars cause inflation rates to skyrocket. For reasons like this, inflation rates, and many other economic measurements, use trimmed means

Sensitivity

Variance and standard deviation are the most common measures of variability, but they are sensitive to outliers
